Everything a CA firm needs.
In one place.

Eight tightly integrated modules built specifically for the realities of Indian CA practice — compliance deadlines, GST workflows, multi-client management, and team coordination.

Client Management

Track clients by entity type, PAN, status, risk — with import/export and group management

Task & Compliance

Deadline-driven task engine with priority tracking, assignment, and compliance monitoring

GST Invoicing

Create tax invoices with HSN/SAC codes, auto GST calculation, recurring billing support

Smart Dashboard

Real-time overview of overdue tasks, outstanding revenue, upcoming deadlines, and team activity

Document Vault

Secure document storage organized by client, with upload, versioning, and quick access

Financial Reports

Outstanding tracking, revenue forecasts, expense management, and non-compliance alerts

Integrations
